Explore the world of dogs, learn about different breeds, and have fun!

Welcome to our website dedicated to everything about dogs. Whether you're a dog lover, owner, or just curious, we've got something for you. Get started now!

You can play a quiz game and get points to vote on different breeds. You have 3 chances to guess to breed. If you guess it right, you get three voting points and if you don't you lose one.

The gallery tab has almost all of the breeds of dogs. You can also choose your favorite dogs and browse through their pictures in a separate tab.
